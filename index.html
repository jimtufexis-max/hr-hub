What's Inside

Every Edge,
One Screen.

Pull up a game and see every signal in seconds — from barrel rate to bullpen exposure.

⚾

Statcast Anchor Model

Barrel%, Pull-Air%, Hard Hit%, ISO — blended with actual HR/PA outcome rate as sample grows. The most predictive public metrics, properly weighted.

🎯

3×3 Zone Matchups

Batter power zones vs pitcher vulnerable zones from the same Statcast feed. Select any pitch type to see the specific overlap — no black box.

⚡

Arsenal × BvP Analysis

Batter ISO vs each pitch type in the SP's mix, weighted by usage. A pitcher with a dominant slider matters more when you actually struggle against sliders.

🌡️

Heat & Drought Tags

🌋 Due when 5+ of 6 heat signals converge — barrel event, above-avg Barrel%, Poisson drought, HR-friendly park and SP. Regression-to-the-mean, quantified.

💰

Bet Tracker + ROI

Track bets with American odds, build parlays, log wins/losses. See edge tier (S/A/B/C), P&L, ROI by tier, and model calibration against your real results.

🧠

Auto-Calibration

After 15 resolved bets, the model automatically adjusts displayed probabilities based on your hit rate vs model expectations. Gets sharper over time.

📊

Statcast Percentile Bars

10 percentile bars per player: xwOBA, xBA, xSLG, EV, Hard Hit%, K%, BB%, Barrel%, Sweet Spot%, Sprint Speed — computed live from the same distribution.

🌦️

Weather + Park Factors

Handedness-adjusted park factors, wind direction, temperature, altitude. Coors Field only boosts home hitters — away splits deflated separately.

🔒

Zero Backend, Zero Cost

Runs entirely in your browser. Data comes directly from Statcast + MLB APIs via a Cloudflare Worker proxy. No account, no subscription, no data stored.

// Step 1: Statcast anchor
anchor = 0.010
  + barrel% × 0.042
  + pull_air% × 0.018
  + hard_hit% × 0.003
  + (barrel × pull) × 0.25

// Step 2: Signal logit stack
lo = logit(anchor)
  + pitcher × 0.38
  + platoon × 0.22
  + park_wx × 0.25
  + tto × 0.28
  + ... 7 more signals

// Step 3: Per-game conversion
perPA = sigmoid(lo) // cap: 7%
prob = 1-(1-perPA)^expPA
