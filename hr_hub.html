🧮 How the Model Works
DingerIQ uses a Bayesian log-odds model combining 12 weighted signals:

Core (heaviest weight)
• Barrel% × Pull-Air% interaction — best HR predictor
• Season HR/PA anchor — career context
• Platoon matchup — handed advantage

Context
• Park factor × weather multiplier
• Pitcher HR/9 (recent + season blend)
• Arsenal matchup — batter vs pitch-type data

Situational
• Recent momentum (L7, L14, L30 rates)
• Lineup position (leadoff = +10% PA estimate)
• Umpire historical HR rate
• Career BvP vs today's starter
• Day/night splits

Expected range: 5–22% per game. League average ~9%.
