⚡ Probability Formula
Prob = Anchor × Platoon × Pitcher × Park/Wx × Momentum
Anchor — Bayesian-blended HR rate: season 50% + career 30% + L100 20%. Small samples regress toward 3.5% MLB avg to prevent noise.
Platoon — 3 layers: ① handedness prior ② player's actual split rate ③ arsenal skill modifier (batter ISO vs SP's pitch mix vs lg avg).
Pitcher — 2 layers: ① SP HR/9 quality signal (Bayesian shrunk) ② arsenal matchup modifier (fixes Brandon Lowe problem).
Park/Wx — Park factor × wind direction × temperature effect.
Momentum — L15 HR rate + last-3G streak. Regressed to mean.
