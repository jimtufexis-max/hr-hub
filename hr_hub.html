⚾ Pitch Type Key
Fastballs
FF 4-Seam
⚡ Power
High velo, straight. Most common pitch in MLB.
SI Sinker
↓ GB machine
Sinking action, induces ground balls. Low HR risk.
FC Cutter
✂ Cuts late
Hard cut away from opposite-hand hitters.
FS Splitter
↓ K pitch
Drops sharply out of zone. High swing-and-miss.
Breaking Balls
SL Slider
↔ Side break
Lateral break. Effective vs same-hand batters.
ST Sweeper
🌀 Wide break
Wide horizontal sweep. Modern slider variant.
CU Curveball
↓ 12-6 drop
12-6 downward drop. Hard to elevate for HRs.
KC Knuckle-Curve
↓ Biting
Sharper curve variant. Less common.
Offspeed
CH Changeup
🐌 Slow drop
Fastball look, 8-12mph slower. Cross-hand matchup = elevated HR risk.
FO Forkball
↓ Fork drop
Rare split-finger variant. Deep drop, mostly Japanese-trained pitchers.
