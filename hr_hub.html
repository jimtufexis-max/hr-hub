🧮 How the Model Works
Log5 + Bayesian log-odds model v8.2 · 18 weighted signals

⚓ Step 1 — Log5 Base Probability (Bill James)
The model starts with the Log5 formula — a statistically rigorous way to combine batter ability, pitcher ability, and league context into a single true matchup probability:
P = (B × P_p × (1−L)) / (B×P_p×(1−L) + (1−B)×(1−P_p)×L)
where B = batter HR/PA · P_p = pitcher HR/PA allowed · L = MLB avg HR/PA (~3.5%)

🖐️ Handedness Splits in Log5
B uses the batter's hand-specific HR/PA vs today's pitcher handedness — not just the overall rate. Shrunk toward overall rate by sample size (full trust at 300 split PA, min 50 PA). Small-sample batters fall back to the overall anchor automatically.

⚓ Step 2 — Anchor Signals (blended into B)
• xSLG + xwOBA Statcast expected stats (40% of anchor weight)
• Barrel% × Pull-Air% · season HR/PA · prior season blend
• Launch angle zone — ideal 20–35° band · excess or deficit penalized
• Blast rate (EV ≥ 95 mph + angle 20–35°) — best single-swing HR predictor
• Rolling EV trend — L14 spike ≥2 mph above season avg → +4%/mph (cap ±7%)

🔀 Platoon (weight 0.10 — residual after Log5)
Log5 already encodes the hand-specific HR/PA. This residual weight captures the arsenal platoon interaction — the pitcher's actual pitch mix effectiveness vs each hand (xwOBA 55% + ISO 45%) — a signal orthogonal to the raw split rate.
• Population priors: LHB vs LHP 0.72× · RHB vs LHP 1.22× · Switch hitters 1.17× (always bat cross-hand)

⚾ Pitcher (weight 0.18 — residual after Log5)
Log5 already encodes the pitcher's raw HR/PA allowed. This residual weight captures:
• Arsenal matchup — batter xwOBA + ISO vs each pitch type vs league avg
• SP fly-ball rate — GB% suppresses HR, extreme FB% adds exposure
• Recent form — last 3 starts K/BB/HR trend → ±15%
• Fatigue flag — 100+ pitches last outing → +8%
• K% suppressor — high-strikeout pitchers (K/9 >9) generate fewer batted balls: −1.8% per extra K/9, capped −12%
• Sample-dependent trust — early-season pitchers (≤5 starts) get 15-25% trust; full trust at 15-20 starts. Prevents over-reacting to small samples.
• Whiff rate · BB rate · Rest days · Barrel% allowed

🏟️ Park × Weather (weight 0.25)
• Park factor (per-ballpark, hand-adjusted) · Temperature
• Wind: direction + speed aligned to power alleys (blowing out ≥15 mph ≈ +18%)

📋 Situational Modifiers
• Team HR context — team HR rate vs league avg (boosts stacks, flags cold lineups)
• Career BvP — recency weighted: this season 1.0× · last season 0.55× · career 0.25×
• Times through order · Umpire HR rate
• Confirmed lineup position (empirical PA by slot) · Home/Away splits
• Plate discipline · Bullpen exposure

💰 EV Formula
Fair odds = ((1 − P) / P) × 100
EV% = (P × decimal odds) − 1
Shown on every player detail card when FD odds are loaded.
